About Oude Mollen Food Garden

It began as a humble, dilapidated piece of land that captured the keen eye of the founder who was a gentleman that teamed up with the surrounding community to turn the space into a garden.

It also functioned as an environment for the founder to run a program for young impoverished teenagers from tough backgrounds, who would come and work in the garden as a means of keeping them off the streets and developing them through mentorship.

The garden has been a means of recreation and comfort for mentally ill patients admitted in Valkenberg Hospital. At Oude Mollen Food Garden we run a kids programme to teach them the basic tools they need to become impactful individuals
